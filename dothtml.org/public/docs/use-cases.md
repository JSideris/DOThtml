### When to choose DOThtml?

DOThtml is uniquely suited for scenarios where you need modern UI capabilities without the overhead or architectural constraints of a traditional framework.

*   **Game Engines**: Build your HUD, menus, and inventory systems in DOThtml while your engine handles the high-performance canvas rendering.
*   **Legacy System Modernization**: Add modern, reactive components to jQuery, PHP, or Rails sites without a full rewrite.
*   **Embedded Widgets & SaaS Snippets**: Build chat bots, feedback tools, or "Buy Now" buttons that are safe to embed on any website thanks to Shadow DOM isolation.
*   **Browser Extensions**: Create high-performance, low-footprint UIs for the web's most resource-constrained environments.
*   **IoT & Embedded Interfaces**: Build lightweight, reactive configuration portals for devices with limited CPU and memory.
*   **Micro-Frontends**: Share UI components across different teams and stacks with minimal friction.
